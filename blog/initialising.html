Initialising

I have always had issues writing. This particular form of communication has always seemed quite alien to me. But it is now time for me to improve this particular skill mostly because I have realised that it is quite an important ability to master in the long run. There are multiple benefits of being able to express yourself efficiently both academically and otherwise.

Who am I ?

I am a Law Student in India pursuing matters of Constitutional Law and Social Justice. I have a keen interest in philosophy and sociology too, in addition to an interest in Lacanian and Freudian psychology.

What do I indend to do here ?

I wish to use this particular platform as a way to not only develop on my writing skills but also develop on my own aspects of research and academic inquiry. I indend to write on matters of law, philosophy and politics, and the interesting interconnections between them. The posts may be opinionated which I invite you to question and deconstruct. I also intend to maintain a regularity with respect to the frequency with which I publish here. I also would appriciate an open dialog between Me and You (yes you the reader!) so feel free to email me if you have anything to say about the topics that I have written about.
